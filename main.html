Jana Nayagan(transl. ”People's Hero” or “Democrat”) is an upcoming 2026 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film[4] directed by H. Vinoth and produced by KVN Productions. The film stars Vijay, Pooja Hegde and Bobby Deol in the lead roles, alongside Mamitha Baiju, Gautham Vasudev Menon, Prakash Raj, Narain and Priyamani It is intended to be the final film appearance of Vijay before his entry into politics, and KVN's first Tamil film production.
